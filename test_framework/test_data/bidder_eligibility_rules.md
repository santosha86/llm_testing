# SPPC Round 7 Bidder Eligibility Requirements

## Section 1: Eligibility Rules

All bidders must meet ALL of the following criteria to be considered eligible:

### Rule 1: Local Content Requirement
- Minimum local content percentage: **30%**
- Calculation based on total project value including equipment and labor

### Rule 2: Financial Rating
- Minimum credit rating: **BBB** (or equivalent from recognized agencies)
- Acceptable agencies: S&P, Moody's, Fitch

### Rule 3: Track Record
- Minimum completed renewable energy projects: **3 projects**
- Projects must be operational for at least 12 months

### Rule 4: Technical Capacity
- Minimum installed capacity experience: **100 MW**
- Combined capacity from all completed projects

### Rule 5: Safety Record
- Maximum lost time injury rate (LTIR): **0.5**
- Based on last 3 years of operations

## Section 2: Additional Requirements

### Performance Bond Trigger
- If proposed tariff is below **1.00 SAR/kWh**, bidder must provide additional performance bond equal to 10% of project value

### Consortium Rules
- Lead partner must meet at least Rules 1, 2, and 3 independently
- Combined consortium must meet all rules
